Who We Are

Hot Beans Web is a forward-thinking web development company specialising in high-performance, energy-efficient websites for businesses across the UK. We focus on clean code, accessibility, responsive design and sustainable hosting solutions. Our mission is to combine digital innovation with environmental responsibility. We train our developers to write optimised code, compress media files, reduce unnecessary scripts and build websites that consume less energy while maintaining excellent user experience.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

The internet contributes significantly to global energy consumption. At Hot Beans Web, we actively reduce this impact by:

• Writing efficient HTML and CSS code
• Minimising JavaScript usage
• Optimising images and media
• Reducing server requests
• Promoting accessible and inclusive design

Sustainability is not just a statement for us — it is embedded into every stage of our development process.
